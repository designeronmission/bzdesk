Privacy Policy

Introduction

Aparajayah Technologies Pvt Ltd owns and operates the websites located at www.BZDesk.com and other related BZDesk web pages (the "Website"). Aparajayah Technologies ("Aparajayah", "we", or "us") understands that you care about how information about you is collected and used. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us.

This Privacy Policy describes our privacy practices with respect to the Website. By using the Website you signify your acceptance of our collection and use of your personal information in the manner and for the purposes described herein. If you do not agree with some aspect of our intended use, please use the methods below to opt out or refrain from using the Website.

Collection of Information

Information You Provide

We may ask you to provide personal information when you visit our Site, use our products, register for a program or service, subscribe to a newsletter, fill out a form, or in connection with other activities. This information may include your name, title, email address, mailing address, phone number, financial information, and other details. In case of technical issues, we might also collect information related to support or service incidents.

Information Collected Automatically

To measure and improve the performance of our Site, we collect information through automatic data collection tools such as web beacons, cookies, embedded links, and pixel tags. These help us track user interactions, customize your experience, and recognize you on subsequent visits. We may also collect data from our social networking pages.

Use of Cookies and Other Web Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device. When you visit our Site, cookies allow us to compile usage information and recognize you on return visits. Pixel tags help us understand which parts of our Site are visited and assess the effectiveness of our communications. If you provide personal information, such as your email address, cookies and tags are associated with that data.

Additionally, some email messages include "click-through URLs" that pass through our server, enabling us to track user interest. Third-party partners like Google Analytics help analyze browsing behavior and provide analytical data to improve the Site's functionality.

Why We Collect Information

We use your information for multiple purposes:

  • To improve customer service and respond to your requests
  • To enhance our products and services
  • To run promotions, contests, surveys, and other features
  • To conduct market research and deliver personalized ads
  • To contact you regarding your account or inquiries
  • To send periodic emails and updates

Aggregated, non-personally identifiable information may also be shared publicly or with our partners for analytical purposes.

How to Opt-Out of Communications

You may opt-in to receive communications from us at various locations on our Site or social media pages. If you wish to unsubscribe, please email privacy@BZDesk.com, follow the "unsubscribe" link provided in our emails, or contact our privacy department.

How We Protect Your Personal Information

Your personal information is stored on secure servers maintained by Aparajayah and our trusted partners. We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your data against loss, theft, and misuse. Data transmitted over the Internet is encrypted using SSL or similar technologies.

While we strive to protect your data, no security control is 100% effective. In the event of a breach, we will take appropriate steps in accordance with applicable laws.

When We Disclose Your Personal Information

General Disclosure

We may share your information with our subsidiaries, affiliates, or strategic partners strictly for purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your information is not sold or rented to third-party marketers.

In Connection with an Acquisition

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or reorganization, your personal data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, which will be required to continue protecting your information in line with this Privacy Policy.

Legal Process or Investigation

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, to protect our rights and property, prevent fraud, or ensure the safety of our users and the public.

Children's Privacy

In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, we do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children under 13. The Site is not intended for children under this age.

Third Party Linked Websites

Our Site and social media pages contain links to external websites. Aparajayah is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of each website you visit.

Accessing and Updating Your Information

We strive to keep your personal information accurate, complete, and current. If you wish to update your information or modify your communication preferences, please email privacy@bzdesk.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. New users will be subject to the updated policy upon posting, while existing users will be notified and given 30 days for any changes to take effect.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your information, please email us at privacy@BZDesk.com.
